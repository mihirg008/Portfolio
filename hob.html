Interests

Currently I am associated to 2 societies in IIT Kharagpur :

  • Team Kart:
    I am currently a Trainee at Team Kart.
    It is a research group set up in the Institute under the department of Mechanical Engineering and works on designing, manufacturing and testing formula based car to participate and represent IIT Kharagpur in various National and International events.

  • Gopali Youth Welfare Society:
    I am currently a Junior Executive Member here.
    It is a non-govenmental organization run by IIT Kharagpur students and some faculty members with support from local people.
    Here we aim to improve the living standard of the people of Gopali and also provide quality education to the children of that area. We also organize camps to grow awareness among people on various social issues.
